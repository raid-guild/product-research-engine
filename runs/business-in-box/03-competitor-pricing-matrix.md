## Positioning Gaps

1. "Too simple for Jobber, too operational for QuickBooks."
2. "Set up for you this week" rather than "learn a platform."
3. "Accountant-ready records" without acting as tax preparer.
4. "One job timeline" that captures inquiry, photos, quote, invoice, payment, follow-up, and receipts.
5. "Admin recovery" for businesses already operating in chaos.

## Differentiation Opportunities

- Concierge onboarding plus automation: import old customers, set up templates, connect payments, create first reports.
- Narrow vertical defaults: handyman estimate templates, job categories, photo notes, deposit/final invoice flows.
- CPA packet export: income summary, expense summary, uncategorized items, receipts, invoice/payment ledger, mileage placeholder.
- Review and referral loop: after payment, ask for review; after review, ask for referral.
- Lightweight "business health inbox": unpaid invoices, stale quotes, missing receipts, jobs without follow-up.
