# Compliance Boundary Notes

## Product Boundary

The product may organize business operations and records. It should not provide legal advice, tax advice, tax preparation, or professional bookkeeping judgment unless delivered through qualified partners under their own engagement terms.

## Safe Product Capabilities

The product can:

- Provide general business setup checklists.
- Link to official federal, state, and local sources.
- Store formation documents, EIN letters, licenses, insurance certificates, invoices, receipts, and contracts.
- Track income and expenses based on user input or user-approved rules.
- Categorize transactions into broad buckets with user review.
- Generate summaries, exports, and accountant-ready packets.
- Remind users about dates, missing records, unsigned documents, unpaid invoices, and uncategorized expenses.
- Route users to a CPA, bookkeeper, attorney, registered agent, payroll provider, or official agency.
- Explain that rules vary by state/locality and that the user should consult qualified professionals.

## Unsafe Or High-Risk Capabilities

The product should not:

- Recommend a legal entity as personalized legal advice.
- Tell a user which tax election to make.
- Tell a user which deductions they should claim.
- Prepare, sign, or file tax returns unless through a qualified tax-preparer partner flow.
- Hold itself out as an attorney, CPA, enrolled agent, tax preparer, or bookkeeper.
- Make local licensing determinations for specific jobs.
- Provide review gating, fake reviews, or incentives that violate review platform/FTC rules.
- Give employment classification advice for employees vs contractors.
- Recommend insurance coverage as professional advice.

## Language Guidelines

Use:

- "Organize your records."
- "Prepare a packet for your accountant."
- "Track income and expenses."
- "User-reviewed categorization."
- "General information, not legal or tax advice."
- "Rules vary by state and locality."
- "Consult a qualified professional."

Avoid:

- "We optimize your taxes."
- "We tell you which entity to choose."
- "We make you compliant."
- "Guaranteed deduction."
- "Tax strategy."
- "Legal protection."
- "We file for you."

## Risky Feature List

| Feature | Risk | Safer Version |
|---|---|---|
| Entity recommendation quiz | Legal advice risk | Education-only comparison with official links and attorney referral. |
| Deduction finder | Tax advice risk | Receipt organizer with user-approved categories and CPA export. |
| Tax filing | Preparer/PTIN/e-file risk | Partner CPA/tax preparer handoff. |
| License checker | Legal/regulatory risk | Checklist with official state/local source links and user confirmation. |
| Contractor classification helper | Employment/legal/tax risk | General education and professional referral. |
| Review automation | FTC/platform risk | Neutral review request to all eligible customers; no fake reviews, no suppression. |

## Operating Controls

- Add clear disclaimers in onboarding and reports.
- Require user approval for categorization changes.
- Keep audit trails for imports, edits, and exports.
- Separate "general education" from "professional advice."
- Use partner terms for CPA/bookkeeper/attorney services.
- Do not let AI answer legal/tax questions directly; route to safe informational responses and sources.
