# Support Automation Opportunities

## Support Strategy

The product should avoid becoming a hidden service business. Use concierge onboarding to learn, but turn repeated support into product flows quickly.

The support posture should be:

- Configure once.
- Automate routine reminders.
- Escalate exceptions.
- Route regulated questions out.

## Highest-Leverage Automations

| Area | Automation | Support Burden Reduced |
|---|---|---|
| Onboarding | Guided import from CSV, phone contacts, Square/Stripe/Wave exports, or manual spreadsheet. | Reduces setup calls and data cleanup. |
| Templates | Vertical-specific estimate, invoice, reminder, review request, and receipt-request templates. | Reduces "what do I write?" questions. |
| Records packet | Monthly auto-generated packet with missing-item checklist. | Reduces tax-season panic and manual report requests. |
| Inbox | Stale quote, unpaid invoice, missing receipt, job without follow-up. | Reduces owner forgetting and support nudges. |
| AI drafts | Customer replies, estimate summaries, invoice reminders, review requests. | Reduces blank-page friction. |
| Categorization | Suggested categories with user approval and recurring rules. | Reduces manual data entry while preserving boundary. |
| Help center | Contextual help tied to current page/task. | Reduces generic support questions. |
| Partner routing | "Ask a CPA/bookkeeper/attorney" handoff. | Prevents regulated support creep. |

## Support-Avoidant Product Patterns

- Require setup fee so high-touch onboarding is economically visible.
- Cap concierge cleanup hours.
- Use checklists with progress state instead of open-ended chat.
- Make every recurring support request a candidate for automation.
- Add "I do not know, ask a professional" paths for legal/tax questions.
- Track unresolved support categories weekly.

## First Support Metrics

- Time to first invoice sent.
- Time to first payment reminder configured.
- Number of jobs imported per customer.
- Percentage of customers generating monthly packet.
- Number of support tickets per active customer.
- Manual minutes per customer in onboarding.
- Manual minutes per customer per month after onboarding.

## Support Red Flags

Kill or narrow if:

- Average monthly manual support exceeds 60 minutes/customer after month two.
- Customers keep asking for tax/legal advice as the core value.
- Customers cannot complete routine tasks without founder help.
- Each vertical requires heavily custom workflows.
- Price resistance prevents charging for onboarding.

## Recommended Support Tooling

Early:

- Shared onboarding checklist.
- Loom-style walkthroughs.
- Canned response library.
- Support tags for pain categories.
- Monthly "missing records" auto-email.

Later:

- In-app assistant constrained to product help and general education.
- Workflow diagnostics.
- AI-generated help article suggestions from support logs.
- Partner directory and referral flow.
