# Dossier File Guide

Use this guide when creating a full research dossier after the signal gate.

## `00-product-intro-brief.md`

Compact final product brief after research. Include recommended wedge, buyer/user, first promise, and initial feature set.

## `01-market-opportunity-memo.md`

Executive summary of market attractiveness, core thesis, market signals, recommended segment, positioning, first offer, risks, decision, and sources.

## `02-segment-ranking-scorecard.md`

Rank likely segments or verticals. Include scoring criteria, top 3 notes, and rationale for avoiding weaker options.

## `03-competitor-pricing-matrix.md`

Compare direct competitors, adjacent tools, services, and manual substitutes. Include pricing, target user, strengths, gaps, and source links.

## `04-customer-pain-map.md`

Map workflow, pain points, urgency, current workarounds, must-solve items, nice-to-have items, and willingness triggers.

## `05-recommended-icp.md`

Define primary ICP, firmographics or demographics, buyer/user profile, buying triggers, exclusions, expansion path, and buyer message.

## `06-mvp-package-recommendation.md`

Define first product scope, non-scope, package or offer options, no-build test, first 30-day plan, and first success outcome.

## `07-gtm-test-plan.md`

Name the first 3 channel tests, outreach scripts, landing page angles, GTM risk assessment, and partner-channel shortlist.

## `08-customer-interview-script.md`

Include respondent screener, interview goals, opening, current workflow, pain discovery, current tools, message testing, pricing/adoption test, closing, validation scorecard, and synthesis template.

## `09-risk-boundary-notes.md`

Document product boundaries, safe capabilities, unsafe claims or features, language guidelines, risky feature list, and operating controls.

## `10-support-automation-opportunities.md`

Identify support strategy, highest-leverage automations, support-avoidant product patterns, metrics, red flags, and tooling.

## `11-partner-strategy-recommendation.md`

Define partner thesis, priority partner types, partner offer, partner models, sample outreach, first 30-day partner test, and long-term partner direction.

## `12-continue-narrow-pivot-kill-recommendation.md`

Make the final call. Include decision table, reasons to continue, reasons to narrow, validation gates, pivot gates, kill gates, and final recommendation.
